Who We Are

We're a creative team of designers and eco-enthusiasts dedicated to transforming the ordinary into the extraordinary. Our mission is to create delightful, sustainable products that challenge perception - things that aren't quite what they seem at first glance. From planters that look like vintage books to solar lights disguised as classic mason jars, we believe in making sustainability fun and surprising.

Our Core Values

Creativity

We transform everyday objects into unexpected delights, challenging perceptions and bringing joy through design.

Sustainability

Every product is crafted with environmental consciousness, using eco-friendly materials and sustainable practices.

Wonder

We believe in creating moments of surprise and delight through clever, dual-purpose designs.
